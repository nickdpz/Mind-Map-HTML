Uses

  • It is used for expressions that are of a routine and are repeated regularly.

  • It is used to express opinions and feelings.

  • It is used for actions that are scheduled.

Rules

  • With verbs ending in o, ch, sh -es is added;

  • With the verbs ending in consonant + y, the latter is transformed into ie before adding the -s.

  • If the verb ends in a vowel + y, you can add the -s without transforming it.

  • To modal verbs, such as can, may, might or must, never add an -s. They remain unchanged in all their forms.

Be

BE Sentence
I. I am. 
He, She, It. He is, She is, It is.
You, We, They. You are, We are, They are.

Have

Have Sentence
I, You, We, They I have/ I have got
He, She, It He has/ He has got
